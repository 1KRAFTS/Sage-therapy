Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does online therapy work?
    • Online therapy connects you with a licensed therapist through secure video, phone, or chat sessions, allowing you to receive support from the comfort of your home.
  • Is online therapy effective?
    • Yes, research shows that online therapy can be as effective as in-person sessions for many mental health concerns.
  • How do I get started?
    • Simply contact us to schedule an initial consultation. We'll guide you through the process step by step.
